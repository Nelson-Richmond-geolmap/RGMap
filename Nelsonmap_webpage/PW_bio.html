PAUL  WOPEREIS

Paul Wopereis is a Senior Engineering Geologist working with the Geotechnical Team with BECA Ltd in Nelson. He is involved in a range of geotechnical work, including land development, infrastructure projects and natural hazards assessment.  

Paul has worked as an Engineering Geologist in New Zealand for the past 20 years. Prior to this he worked in mineral exploration and mining in New Zealand, Australia and South America for 18 years.
